Mission Statement

Roanoque Baroque serves as an outreach ministry of St. John Lutheran Church to present compelling musical performances of high artistic value to the glory of God, bringing together a diverse range of musicians, audiences, and settings.

Vision Statement

Roanoque Baroque creates a community of musicians who collaborate to form instrumental and vocal ensembles of various sizes and configurations to perform musical masterpieces from the Renaissance to Contemporary periods with an emphasis on the music of the 18th century. Blending professional and dedicated amateur performers, their programs take advantage of settings of acoustical, architectural, spiritual and cultural significance to connect with a wide variety of audiences and build an ever-increasing interest in and appreciation for the historic music of the church and related masterpieces as they demonstrate a witness to the wonder and beauty of creation. A particular emphasis is made to reach out to young musicians and audiences, and those with historically less access to the performing arts including, but not limited to, those who are challenged by hospitalization, incarceration, and mobility and financial constraints. Both musicians and audience members are drawn into closer community with the church and the gospel through their interactions.
